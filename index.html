About this place

A clever little home base.

Welcome to my newly renovated tiny apartment — a compact studio with everything you need to explore Athens.

It sits in Pangrati: a cosy, lively and safe central neighbourhood, within walking distance of the city centre, the National Gallery, museums and parks. You get your own private street entrance, a comfortable double bed, air conditioning, a proper little workspace, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom.

The building has a reinforced steel-frame door and natural airflow, and the studio was fully renovated in summer 2024.

One honest note: it is a genuinely tiny apartment, and the bathroom ceiling is low — you may need to duck. It's flagged here for the same reason guests keep mentioning it: everything else is exactly as described, spotless and thoughtfully put together.

04 — The neighbourhood

Pangrati, on foot.

Leafy, local and safe, Pangrati is full of cafés, tavernas and bars — with the National Gallery and the National Garden practically on the doorstep. Two metro stations and the X95 airport bus are minutes away, and you can walk almost everywhere in central Athens.

  • National Gallery~300 m
  • Evangelismos metro~5 min walk
  • Megaro Mousikis metro~9 min walk
  • Syntagma Square~15 min
  • National Garden~20 min walk
  • X95 bus to the airport~5 min walk
  • Old Town & the sights~35 min walk

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Where is the studio located?
In Pangrati, a central, leafy and safe neighbourhood of Athens — a short walk from the National Gallery and the National Garden, and about five minutes from the metro.
How do I get there from the airport?
The X95 express bus (Airport–Syntagma) stops a few minutes' walk away, and a taxi from the airport takes roughly 40 minutes.
How many guests does it sleep?
It's a studio for up to two guests, with one comfortable double bed.
Is it really that tiny?
Yes — it's a compact micro-studio and the bathroom ceiling is low, so you may need to duck. Everything you need is there, cleverly arranged, and guests consistently find it clean and comfortable.
What's included — AC, Wi-Fi, a kitchen?
Yes to all: air conditioning and heating, fast Wi-Fi with Ethernet, a dedicated workspace, and a fully equipped kitchenette with a fridge, hob, microwave and Nespresso.
How do I check in and book?
Check-in is self-service via a lockbox right outside your own private street entrance, any time you arrive. To check dates and book, send an enquiry using the form below, or email me directly at zach.ermis@gmail.com.
Is the host reachable, and do I get local tips?
Yes — your host is on standby and reachable by phone throughout your stay, with laminated info cards around the flat for anything you need. When you book, you'll also receive a personal “Athens like a local” guide.
